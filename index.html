How this is calculated

Because your full-time salary (£30,000–£35,000) already uses up your Personal Allowance, all reselling profit is taxable at 20% on top of your employment income. This is a slight over-estimate if your combined income approaches £50,270. You will need to declare this via Self Assessment if your reselling profit exceeds £1,000 in a tax year.
